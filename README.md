The Agricultural Expert System for Crop Disease Diagnosis is a rule-based intelligent system designed to assist farmers and agricultural extension officers in identifying crop diseases and recommending appropriate treatments. 
The system will operate using a knowledge base composed of facts and rules such as crop type
(e.g., maize, cassava, cocoa), observable symptoms (e.g., yellow leaves, brown spots, wilting,
stunted growth), and environmental conditions. Rules will encode expert knowledge by linking
combinations of symptoms to specific diseases. 
The system will also provide treatment recommendations. These may include
suggested pesticides, organic control methods, crop rotation practices, or soil improvement
strategies. The proposed system demonstrates key artificial intelligence concepts, including
knowledge representation, rule-based reasoning, inference mechanisms, and decision support,
aligning with modern trends in smart farming and precision agriculture.
